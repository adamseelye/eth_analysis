Ethereum Analysis

This app was created with the purpose of reading various statistics from the Ethereum blockchain.

The data is sourced from etherscan.io using a free API key.


We may view the Ethereum total cryptocurrency supply (in Gwei), check currency and gas prices, and get block and node counts.

Most snapshot dates are at on a static date in 2019, however the Total Supply and Gas Prices are current.

The ability to select dates for all data queries will be available in the future.
